Overview:

The CVE Global Readiness Lead shares high-level technical details of upcoming projects with CVE Site Readiness Leads and CVE Site Infrastructure Leads to estimate basic platform needs for infrastructure analysis. The CVE Site Infrastructure Lead then analyzes the site's ability to support the projected systems, checking power, thermals (PCW), HVAC (cooling), OFA (Oil-Free-Air), and network capabilities. Any risks or gaps identified are reported to the CVE Site Readiness Lead, who then reports program or roadmap risks to the CVE Global Readiness Lead and CVE Global Capacity Lead to determine actions to address or mitigate the issues, such as rebalancing platform counts, relocating platforms, or requesting lab upgrades.
